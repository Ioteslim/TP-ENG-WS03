Our Workshop Safety Rules

Workshop safety is everyone's responsibility, the following rules have been put in place to ensure the safety of all students and staff. Please read the safety rules carefully and submit Safety Awareness Form to our staff before working in this workshop.

  1. Remove all rings, watches & bracelets.
  2. Wear proper shoes.
  3. Long hair should be neatly tied & bundled up, or wearing of cap.
  4. Skirts, Bermudas, three-quarter pants, scarf & sleeveless top are not allowed.
  5. Loose clothing is to be neatly tucked-in, or wearing a dust coat.
  6. Wear safety goggles/ safety classes at all times while working on machines.
  7. Carry out instructions properly & report any unsafe conditions.
  8. Use correct tools and equipment.
  9. Keep workplace clean & tidy at all times.
  10. Have all injuries, however slight, properly attended to.
  11. Do not try to operate a machine unless its operation is fully understood.
  12. Stop machine completely, before making any measurement or adjustment.
  13. Never attempt to stop any rotating spindle of any machine with your hands.
  14. Do not allow any machine to run unattended.
  15. Only ONE student is allowed to operate a machine at any one time.
  16. Do nost horseplay or distract others.
  17. Use only those tools you are authorised to use.
  18. Don't leave tools on the floor, or where they can fall on people.
  19. Approach duty staff when handling hazardous chemical substances.
  20. Always ensure that hazardous substances are handled using proper equipment.
  21. No using of mobile phone in workshop.
  22. Obey all safety rules & signs.

Tool Loans

Tool kits and Drill kits are available for loan either on a daily basis or longer term. The tool kits give a good range of high quality hand tools to cover general day to day workshop use. More specialised tools are available at the service counter.

When you borrow a tool kit you must show your Matriculation Card and have signed the online tool agreement form (here). Staff will check the toolkit is correctly equipped and your loan will be recorded on our central database.

Returning the kits. Please dust off the tools and box. If anything has broken let the staff know when you return the kit. If it's reasonable wear and tear you won't be charged. If you lose items however we will ask you to pay a $$ fine for small items and up to $$ for expensive items. The toolkit is your responsibility while on loan. If the kit is lost or stolen you will be asked to pay a $$$ fine to cover the full replacement cost.
